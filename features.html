Everything You Need, All in One Place

At GoZagel, we believe shipping should be simple. That’s why we’ve built an all-in-one solution that takes the headache out of managing orders, couriers, and shipments—so you can focus on growing your business, not managing logistics.

One Dashboard for All Your Orders

No more jumping between different platforms just to manage orders! GoZagel centralizes your entire order process in a single dashboard. Whether it’s from your online store, social media sales, or manually entered orders, you’ll see everything in one place.

  • ● Seamless Order Syncing: Orders from platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, Salla, and others are automatically pulled into your dashboard.

  • ● Social Media Sales Made Simple: Use GoZagel’s custom shipping links to collect orders from social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Tiktok or WhatsApp and keep everything organized.

  • ● Manual Order Entry: Need to add an order manually? No problem, enter it quickly and watch it sync into your order flow.

All Your Courier Accounts, One Control Hub

Managing multiple courier accounts has never been easier. With GoZagel, you can connect all your courier accounts and handle everything in one platform.

  • ● Connect 40+ Couriers: GoZagel integrates with top couriers like DHL, FedEx, Aramex, J&T, Bosta and more. You can easily compare rates and services.

  • ● Unified Courier Management: Whether you’re using your own courier accounts or GoZagel’s discounted rates, you’ll be able to generate labels, track shipments, and manage couriers from a single dashboard—no need to switch between different courier websites.

  • ● Rate Comparisons Made Easy: Choose the best shipping option for each order by comparing courier rates and services side by side.

Order Management Made Easy

You’re in control of the entire fulfillment process. GoZagel’s simple, intuitive order management makes it easy to stay organized—no technical expertise required.

  • ● Review & Confirm Orders: Manage and confirm orders with just a few clicks. Control what’s ready to ship by marking orders as confirmed or unfulfilled based on customer responses.

  • ● Handle Returns Effortlessly: Process returns just as easily as new orders—all from the same dashboard.

  • ● Streamline Operations: Simplify your workflow with a platform that helps you handle everything from order creation to fulfillment, in one place.

Flexible Shipping Options

Shipping shouldn’t be a hassle. That’s why we offer flexible courier options that fit your needs:

  • ● GoZagel’s Discounted Rates: Access exclusive discounted shipping rates with trusted courier partners.

  • ● Connect Your Own Couriers: Prefer to use your existing courier accounts? No problem—GoZagel lets you link them directly, so you can keep shipping with your trusted partners.

  • ● The flexibility to choose what works for you is key: Whether you’re shipping locally or internationally, you’re in control of how and when you ship.

Real-Time & Centralized Shipment Tracking

Track all your shipments in one place with real-time updates. Stay on top of your deliveries and automatically keep your customers informed on their package status.

  • ● Real-Time Tracking: See where all your packages are at any time, regardless of which courier you’re using.

  • ● Customer Updates: Automatically send shipping updates to your customers to keep them informed and happy.

COD (Cash on Delivery) Reports Made Simple

Keep track of all your Cash on Delivery (COD) payments and ensure you get paid on time. GoZagel simplifies COD reporting, so you always know the status of your COD transactions.

  • ● Accurate COD Tracking: Stay on top of your COD payments by viewing detailed reports on outstanding and received payments.

  • ● Centralized Reporting: Manage COD for all your shipments, no matter which couriers you’re using, from one central location. Keep your finances in check with ease.

Team Access for Smooth Collaboration

Growing your business often means involving more people in the day-to-day operations. GoZagel makes it easy to manage orders and shipments with your team.

  • ● Add Team Members: Invite your team to help manage your orders, shipments, and fulfillment process.

  • ● Custom Permissions: Give your team access to the areas they need, while maintaining control over sensitive information.

  • ● Collaborative Workflow: Your entire team can work together to ensure orders are processed quickly and efficiently, without stepping on each other’s toes.

World-Class Support When You Need It

You’re not alone. Whether you need help setting up your store, figuring out shipping options, or troubleshooting an issue, GoZagel is here for you.

  • ● Chat Support: Have a question? Use our chat to get quick, real-time answers from our friendly team.

  • ● Email Support: Need more detailed assistance? Send us an email, and we’ll be there to help, ensuring you never miss a beat in your business.
