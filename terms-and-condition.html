Security and Payment

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

All payment transactions conducted on this site are fully secured and encrypted. Payments are processed through Cow Pay, utilizing payment systems developed by Fawry Pay and the National Bank of Egypt.

Sender's Liability

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

The sender bears full responsibility for the contents of the shipment, which must comply with applicable local and international laws. Shipping items that violate legal or safety regulations may result in the cancellation or return of the shipment, with a deduction of shipping label issuance fees (30%, capped at 150 EGP), or destruction if deemed hazardous.

Shipping Refunds

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024
  • Customers are entitled to a full refund (100%) for cancelations initiated by the shipping company.
  • For cancelations initiated by the customer prior to shipment departure, a 70% refund is available.

Weight and Dimension Measurement

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and dimensions provided by the sender, with the possibility of re_measurement during transit. Discrepancies may result in additional charges or the return of the shipment with a deduction of 30% (maximum of 150 EGP). If the shipment has already left the sending country, the sender must pay any additional fees, as return is not an option, and failure to pay may lead to the destruction of the shipment.

Packaging

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

The sender is solely responsible for packaging:

  • Document Packaging:

    Documents must be placed in an A4-sized envelope, as size may influence shipping costs.

  • Parcels Packaging:

    Appropriate boxes must be used (substandard or gift boxes that are easily opened are prohibited) to prevent the need for repackaging at the airport or during transit, which could affect shipment size and weight. Ensure contents are securely packed without pressure on the box sides.

Delivery Timeline

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

The delivery period begins the day after the shipment is handed over to the courier and is measured in business days. This period does not include public holidays or customs clearance times. Please note that GoZagel does not guarantee delivery times; therefore, if a shipment does not arrive by the expected delivery date, losses incurred will not be compensated, as delivery depends on flight schedules.

Customs Fees

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

Customs duties or clearance fees may be levied on the recipient, determined by the local customs authority and the shipping company in the destination country, applicable to packages or certain documents like passports. GoZagel, is not responsible for these charges, which are the recipient's obligation. Delays in payment may result in delayed delivery.

Customs Inquiries

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024

The customs authority may request additional information related to the sender or recipient. It is the customer’s responsibility to communicate with customs, as GoZagel cannot respond on the customer’s behalf.

Pickup of Shipments from the Sender

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024
  • A representative will be scheduled to pick up shipments on the next business day following the booking. While the system allows for same-day bookings, please note that this is not guaranteed. If the representative does not arrive, please contact customer service to arrange for the following day.
  • For shipments originating from Egypt, delivery to any branch of the shipping company is prohibited. In such cases, the bill of lading will be considered void, and shipping fees will not be refunded. Additionally, penalties may be imposed for delivery to any branch.
  • When coordinating pickups from centers or villages, please be aware that these areas are not serviced daily. Typically, the representative will arrive within two to three business days, and service may be available only once a week.

Delivery of Shipments to the Recipient

LAST UPDATED: 2/11/2024
  • For shipments arriving in Egypt, delivery is usually made to the recipient's address, with the representative contacting them to arrange the delivery.
  • If the recipient's address is in a village or center, please note that these areas are not serviced daily. The representative typically arrives within two to three business days, and service may be available only once a week.
  • If there are any changes to the recipient's address or mobile number, penalties may be applied. Modifications made after the issuance of the shipping bill will incur additional fees.
