About GoZagel

Royal Works, the proud owner of the "Go Zagel" brand, is a leader in the logistics industry, offering innovative solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of our clients. With our registration in both Egypt and France, we demonstrate our unwavering commitment to quality and reliability in all our services.

The Vision Behind GoZagel

"Go Zagel" embodies our vision of providing comprehensive delivery services that cater to a diverse range of markets. We are dedicated to ensuring that our operations are transparent and efficient, allowing us to offer an exceptional experience for all our partners.

At Go Zagel, we prioritize our customers, ensuring they receive :
High-Quality and Satisfying Shipping Experiences

We strive to deliver excellence at every step.

End-to-End Shipment Tracking

From booking to delivery, our customers are kept informed.

Responsive Customer Feedback Handling

We actively receive and address complaints and suggestions to foster continuous improvement and growth.

Regular Coordination with Shipping Partners

We maintain close communication with our contracted shipping companies to ensure timely and accurate operations.

Comprehensive Staff Training and Support

Our team is equipped to provide quick and effective responses to customer inquiries.

As we look to the future, we are committed to expanding our services and delivering the best logistical solutions in the industry.
